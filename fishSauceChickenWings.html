Fish Sauce Chicken Wings

Don't mistaken this for your regular fried chicken wings! This is Vietnam's take on chicken wings. It has a salty, sweet and spicy flavor as well as brilliant aroma.

25 minutes | 4-6 people

Ingredients

  • 4 oz Fish Sauce
  • 4 oz Water
  • 300 grams Sugar
  • Chicken
  • Your choice of peppers.
