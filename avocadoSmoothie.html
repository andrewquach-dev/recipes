Avocado Smoothie

Although avocado is often used in savory dishes, Vietnam has decided to make a dessert drink out of it. In hotter temperatures, a ice blended smoothie with sliced avocadoes and with some condensed milk can help cool you down.

5 minutes | 2 people

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of ice cubes
  • 1 ripe Avocado
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Condensed milk to taste
