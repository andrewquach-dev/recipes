Salty Fish Fried Rice

It's your typical rendition of fried rice but with a pungent twist. Salty fish bits. Don't let the smell fool you! The dish is made up of a special ingredient and your choice of meat but traditionally people go with chicken.

25 minutes | 4-6 people

Ingredients

  • Salted fish
  • 16 oz of Chicken or your choice of meat
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp Potato Starch
  • 3 cups of steamed rice
  • 2 tsp Fried Salted Fish
  • 1-1/2 tsp Sugar
  • 1/8 tsp White Pepper
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Scallion
